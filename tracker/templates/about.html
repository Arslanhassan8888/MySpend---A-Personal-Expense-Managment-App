We're on a mission to make personal finance management simple, intuitive, and accessible for everyone. Based in Manchester, England, we're building tools that help you take control of your financial future.

Our Story

MySpend was born from a simple observation: managing personal finances should not be complicated. Too many people struggle with budgeting, not because they lack discipline, but because the tools available are either too complex or too simplistic.

We set out to create something different - a finance tracker that is powerful enough for serious budgeters, yet simple enough for anyone to use from day one. Every feature we build is designed with one goal in mind: helping you make better financial decisions.

Today, MySpend serves users across the UK and beyond, helping them track expenses, set budgets, and achieve their financial goals with confidence.
